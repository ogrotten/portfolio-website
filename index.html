Project: BKMK

Individual, anonymous bookmarks.

Nevermind Chrome or Firefox saved bookmarks. That requires you to login to the browers itself, and sometimes you don't want to go through all of that.

bkmk gives unregistered users a simple place to hold quick bookmarks with a randomized, Mad Max style post-apocalyptic gang leader name (Ex: DirtyRoachOfTheStrikerChamps) for a url. Further, by following simple registration (no email required!) and login, users will be allowed to edit the page in the future!

Status: In Process.

Project: Golfitaire

Golf solitaire... taken seriously.

Golf style solitaire is a simple game: match the turned-over card as the next or previous in the series with any face-up card on the table.

Golfitaire takes this concept to the nth degree by laying out the cards in new and exciting patterns. Adorned with serious miniature golf backgrounds and 9, 18, or 27 rounds per game, it will change the way you see solitaire, and golf, forever.
