Project: BKMK

Individual, anonymous bookmarks.

Nevermind Chrome or Firefox saved bookmarks. That requires you to login to the browers itself, and sometimes you don't want to go through all of that.

bkmk gives unregistered users a simple place to hold quick bookmarks with a randomized, Mad Max style post-apocalyptic gang leader name for a url. Further, by following simple registration (no email required!) and login, users will be allowed to edit the page in the future!
